Song
Kasam Jhoothi Pyaar Jhootha
Lyric
Kasam jhoothi…
Pyaar jhootha…
Sab jhootha…
Kaali raaton ki siyaahi mein, tera chehra dhoondta hoon
Yeh veeraniyan deewaron ki, mujhse baatein karti hain
Teri saanson ki woh khushboo, ab hawa mein hai gumshuda
Teri yaadein ek khanjar si, mere seene mein gadti hain
Ek dhund si chhaayi hai, meri aankhon ke saamne
Woh saare lamhe ab toh, lagte hain ik afsaane
Teri kasam bhi jhoothi, tera pyaar bhi jhootha nikla
Jisko maana tha khuda, woh toh bas ek saaya nikla
Mere dil ke aaine mein, tera aks tha jo basaa
Toot kar woh bhi bikhra, har tukda qaatil nikla
Woh kaagaz ki ek kashti, baarishon mein jo chalaayi thi
Woh jhoothe tere waade, jinki maine lau jalaayi thi
Teri hansi ke peechhe, chhupi thi ik gehri saazish
Meri rooh tak ko chhalni kar gayi, woh dhokhe ki baarish
Ek veham tha shaayad mera, jo tujhpe aitbaar kiya
Tere patthar dil ko maine, kyun itna zyaada pyaar kiya
Teri kasam bhi jhoothi, tera pyaar bhi jhootha nikla
Jisko maana tha khuda, woh toh bas ek saaya nikla
Mere dil ke aaine mein, tera aks tha jo basaa
Toot kar woh bhi bikhra, har tukda qaatil nikla
Ab toh tanhaai ka khauf bhi, mera dost ban gaya hai
Yeh andhere mujhse poochhte hain, tera noor kahan kho gaya hai
Kisse shikaayat main karun, jab munsif hi tha bewafa
Meri maut ka farmaan, teri khaamoshi ne likh diya
Mere khwaabon ke mahal ko, tune pal mein hai jala diya
Meri hasti ko mita ke, khud ko kitna bada bana liya
Ab har aahat pe lagta hai, ke tu laut kar aaya hai
Par yeh dil bhi jaanta hai, yeh maut ka bulaawa aaya hai
Tere diye zakhmon par, ab marham nahi lagta
Iss duniya mein ab kuchh bhi, mujhe sachcha nahi lagta
Ek jeeta jaagta laash hoon, jo bas saansein leta hai
Tera naam jab bhi koi, mere saamne leta hai
Teri kasam bhi jhoothi, tera pyaar bhi jhootha nikla!
Jisko maana tha khuda, woh toh bas ek saaya nikla!
Mere dil ke aaine mein, tera aks tha jo basaa!
Toot kar woh bhi bikhra, har tukda qaatil nikla!
Kasam jhoothi…
Pyaar jhootha…
Sab jhootha…
Jhootha…
